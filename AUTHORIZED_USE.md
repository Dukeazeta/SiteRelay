# Authorized use policy

SiteRelay is intended for interfaces you own, are licensed to use, or have
explicit permission to inspect and reproduce.

SiteRelay does not grant rights to source material. A successful browser
capture does not mean that its text, trademarks, images, fonts, code, or visual
design may legally be reused. Users are responsible for reviewing the source's
license and obtaining any required permission.

SiteRelay must not be used to bypass authentication, paywalls, access controls,
anti-bot measures, closed Shadow DOM, cross-origin browser protections, or
technical restrictions imposed by a source.

By default, SiteRelay records asset and font metadata without downloading the
files. Asset downloading is available only when the user explicitly confirms
that they are authorized to reuse those assets. Download manifests preserve the
source URL, MIME type, size, and SHA-256 hash for later review.

The official SiteRelay showcase is original project material and is the
recommended source for demonstrations, tests, tutorials, and judging.
